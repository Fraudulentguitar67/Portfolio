Jonny is a 14 year old photographer based in Utah, he photographs nature, wildlife, and cars. At the young age of 10, Jonny became obsessed with cars and began to photograph them with his phone at the age of 12. He loved doing photography and began to photograph more than just cars and started to drift away from his love for cars, but in the summer of 2022 he went to a car show and loved cars yet again, this soon changed as he began to go on hikes more often and started to photograph wildlife and nature. He still loves to photograph nature and wildlife.
